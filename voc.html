Scroll down to start learning some Ketolish words.

Lesson 1 (My name)

In Ketolish, Nelith means me or my. It mostly depends on the context of the conversation.

Example:

"Nelith thalos Issamoth."

means

"My name is Issam."

in English.

Lesson 2 (Food)

In Ketolish, fruits have a different name than we are commonly used to. For example: Apple means "elvathal" in Ketolish. It doesn't matter if you are talking about a single or multiple apples.

Example:

"Sylvarix nalarith elvathal"

means

"I love to eat apples."

in English.

Lesson 3 (Job)

In Ketolish, jobs usually have the same name. It depends in which sector you work. In this example: Zalnor means Nurse. But it can also mean doctor or surgeon.

Example:

"Nelith takzorin Zalnor arkinath sa'karianth"

means

"I work as a nurse in a hospital."

in English.
